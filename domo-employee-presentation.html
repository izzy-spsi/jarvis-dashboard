Pillar One

Show
Up.

"Early is on time.
On time is late.
Late is unacceptable."
Showing up means two things.

Be here physically — on time, every time, no excuses.

Be here mentally — present, focused, and locked in from the moment you walk through that door.

Your team and your guests are counting on you before you even say a word.
02
🤝
Pillar Two

Hospital-
ity.

"It's not about what you do
for the guest — it's about
how you make them feel."
Notice the word hospital inside hospitality.
That's not a coincidence.

A hospital exists for one reason — to take care of you.

That is exactly what we do here. Every guest who walks through that door is in our care.

They may forget your name. They may forget what they ordered.
But they will never forget how you made them feel.
03
🙌
Pillar Three

Team-
work.

"Show up for your team
the way you want them to
show up for you."
When one person is in the weeds, we all grab plates.

"In the weeds" means slammed — when the rush hits and everything is falling apart at once. Great teams don't point fingers. They grab plates.

When a teammate is struggling, you don't wait to be asked. You step in.
04
💬
Pillar Four

Commu-
nication.

"No communication becomes misinformation.
Misinformation becomes misunderstanding.
Master it."
Communication is the greatest superpower in this building — when mastered.

Mastering communication can be the one superpower that will help you achieve greatness — in this cafe, in your career, and in your life.

Say what you need. Ask for help. Speak up early.

Silence doesn't solve problems. It creates them.
