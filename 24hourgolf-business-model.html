01 — Strategy
The Business Flywheel

24 Hour Golf operates as a multi-layered platform. The facility drives member acquisition. Members generate data. Data powers coaching. Coaching improves retention. Retention funds network expansion. Network expansion creates platform value.

📍
Facilities
AI-managed bays. Zero staff. 24/7 access. TrackMan powered.
👤
Members
Recurring revenue base. 4 tiers. Habit formation. Low churn.
📊
Data
Swing data. Session history. Handicap progression. Usage patterns.
🤖
Platform
AI coaching. Competitive network. Licensing OS. Data marketplace.
Core Strategic Insight: Customers are not buying simulator hours. They are buying outcomes — improvement, competition, convenience, and status. Every revenue stream should be designed around delivering one of these four outcomes. The platform becomes more valuable as more members join, more data is captured, and more locations come online.

Express Walk-In (30-min, $25) does not count against any session allotment — it is a standalone walk-in product separate from membership sessions, available to all tiers and non-members on a first-come basis.

Peak Hour Rule (Member tier only): Members have full 24/7 access but peak windows (weekday evenings 5–9pm + weekends) are availability-based. Performance and above receive priority queue access. This protects premium tiers and creates a natural upgrade incentive without restricting Member access outright.

The Platform Horizon

The membership revenue is the business today. The platform is the business in 5 years. These four assets compound in value as the network scales.

01
Performance Data
Swing metrics, handicap progression, session frequency, equipment response data from 50,000+ members. TrackMan sold for $600M+ — and they don't own the member layer or the facility.
→ License to manufacturers
02
Facility OS
The autonomous operations platform — access control, AI occupancy, remote management, billing integration. License to third-party operators who want to run their own 24HG-style facilities.
→ SaaS to operators
03
Coaching Marketplace
AI coaching today. Human pro marketplace tomorrow. The Peloton instructor model — coaches build audiences, earn income, drive member retention. Platform takes 20–30% of all transactions.
→ Marketplace revenue
04
Golf Identity Network
A real-time, AI-calibrated handicap and performance identity system. The USGA handicap system is 100 years old. 24HG owns the replacement — live data, cross-location, AI-verified.
